Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome Storage API to save your preferences (on/off status) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your computer.

Permissions

The extension requires access to all websites to scan and block spoiler content. No data is collected or transmitted - all checks are performed only on your computer.

Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties because we don't collect information in the first place.

Policy Changes

If we change this policy, we will update the date above. We recommend checking periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact us through GitHub or Chrome Web Store.
