# Chrome Web Store Description

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Blocks spoilers - add your own keywords to filter content across all websites. Works on sports, TV shows, movies, and more.

---

## Full Description (plain LTR - copy this exactly)

Anti-Spoiler automatically hides spoiler content across the web based on your custom keywords. Browse without accidentally seeing results, headlines, or posts that ruin your favorite shows, games, or events.

Features:
- Custom keywords: Add any terms you want to block (sports teams, show names, movie titles, etc.)
- Works everywhere: Scans and blocks spoilers on all websites you visit
- Dynamic content: Detects and hides spoilers in content that loads after the page
- Quick toggle: Turn blocking on or off with one click
- Hebrew and English: Full support for both languages
- Your data stays local: Keywords and settings are stored only on your device

Perfect for:
- Sports fans avoiding game results
- TV and movie fans catching up on shows
- Anyone who wants to browse the web without spoilers

Add your keywords, enable the extension, and browse in peace.
